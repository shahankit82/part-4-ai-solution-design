# Part 4: AI Solution Design for Business Problem

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an AI-based solution design for a real-world telecom business problem. The focus is on identifying a problem, proposing a suitable AI approach, and evaluating its business impact.

---

## 🏢 Domain

Telecom

---

## 🎯 Problem

Customer churn and inefficient support handling lead to revenue loss and poor customer experience.

---

## 🤖 Proposed Solution

An AI system combining:

* Churn prediction (classification model)
* NLP-based ticket classification

---

## 📊 Data Requirements

* Customer usage data
* Billing information
* Support tickets (text)
* Churn labels

---

## 🧠 Model Approach

* Feed-forward neural network (churn prediction)
* LSTM/Transformer (text classification)

---

## 📈 Evaluation

* Accuracy, precision, recall
* Business KPIs (retention rate, cost savings)

---

## ⚠️ Responsible AI

* Bias detection
* Human-in-the-loop validation
* Data privacy compliance
